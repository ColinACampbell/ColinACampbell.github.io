Who am I ?

I'm Colin A. Campbell, a proud CS student and a proud Jamaican who enjoys doing almost about anything that satisfy my interests. But there is nothing I love more than programming, designing and building software applications. With regards to programming I'm always learning and keeping up to pace with the evolution of our changing technology - as I should.

Whether it's for fun, for school, or an idea. I still enjoy the the art of it - by keeping it simple ( even though coding itself is an art). I aspire to be a software engineer and /or software engineer so I've been learning to code from I was 16 years old. I didn't know what I was doing from start and had no intentions of a career in computer science but I sure liked it. Some people call me a 'young prodigy', but I just call myself a good googler. Coding started as a hobby but know its a part of a my life.
